Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our tax, compliance, and registration services.

1 How long does CAC registration take?

Business Name registration typically takes 3–5 working days, while Limited Liability and NGO/Trustee registration can take 7–14 working days depending on CAC workload. At GKI, we prioritize speed and accuracy for faster approval.

2 Do you register UK and US companies remotely?

Yes — we register UK LTD and US LLC companies 100% remotely. You do not need to travel. We also obtain EIN, UBI, Articles of Incorporation, and tax setup documents.

3 Can you help me obtain TIN, SCUML, NAFDAC and other permits?

Yes. GKI processes TIN, Tax Clearance, SCUML, NAFDAC, Business Premise Permit, Export License, ITF, NSITF and other regulatory documents required to operate legally in Nigeria.

1 Are your prices fixed or do they vary by service?

Prices vary depending on the type of service — company registration, international company setup, tax filing, bookkeeping, or licensing. However, our pricing is transparent, and you will always know the cost before we proceed.

2 Do you offer discounts for multiple services?

Yes. If you’re processing two or more services (e.g., CAC + TIN + Tax Clearance), GKI offers a bundled discount. This helps businesses reduce compliance costs.

3 Is there a refund policy if my application is rejected?

Yes. GKI maintains a favorable refund policy. If a rejection happens due to our error, we fix it at no extra cost. If caused by client misinformation, we guide you through resolving it properly.

1 How can I track the progress of my registration or filing?

Once we begin your service, you will receive real-time updates via WhatsApp or email. You can also call anytime to check progress or request documents.

2 Do you offer support after a service is completed?

Yes. GKI provides ongoing support for tax filing, annual returns, bookkeeping, and compliance renewals. We ensure your business stays compliant every year.

3 How quickly does your support team respond?

We respond within minutes during working hours. Our team is available Monday–Saturday, 9am–6pm for calls, messages, and follow-ups.
